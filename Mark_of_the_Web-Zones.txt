--> Zone Identifiers <--
0 - local computer
1 - local intranet
2 - trusted sites
3 - internet
4 - restricted sites

Consider these zones for the Windows SmartScreen and Office Protected View which launch warning pop-ups on the users' computers and will be additionally scrutinized by the user.

Files that are emailed from an internal email account via a compromised Exchange email account are not tagged with the Zone Identifier above.
